The Numitaer

The Numitaer is the governing body of numerous interstellar colonies and planets. It is responsible for the oversight of colonization and terraformation relating to the preservation of its vast populous. These colonies, referred to as comets, are dispersed into the galaxy through careful choice.

Terramancers

Terramancers are scholars of resource and physics (Druid & Artificer multiclass in DnD). They play a significant role in Progenitor and its creation of Comets. Terramancers, or Terrans for short, are responsible for the terraforming capabilities of Comets and guaranteeing a safe arrival for their passengers.
